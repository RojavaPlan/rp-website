This is a revolution. The movement that created it looks back on thousands of years of history that have amounted to the most progressive social experiment of our times. Don't expect things here to go your way. It is important to understand the culture and philosophy behind this new emerging society. Things here might be done differently from the way you're used to. Are you willing to learn a lot before you start doing things yourself? Are you ready to cooperate with people from many different cultures? Good! You can help a lot here!

 

What You Can Do Here

It is important that everybody able and willing comes here to participate and make this revolution happen.

Do you speak Kurmanci, Arabic, Aramaean or Turkish and other widely spoken languages? Are you Kurdish, but live abroad? Rojava needs you! We need specialist knowledge translated into Kurmanci from various languages. We need people who can make reports on the situation here for foreign language media. We need people who can teach internationally spoken languages so Rojavan people can speak for themselves rather than being spoken about.

If you have specialist knowledge in expertise fields, particularly media or technology, there is a huge wealth of opportunities for you here to participate from training to organising and supporting others and working with the Rojavan institutions on exciting new projects.

But most of all, your motivation is more important than your skills. Anyone with drive can learn anything. We live together communally. Friendly, relaxed, revolutionary environment.

We also need street artists, musicians and cultural people. Here there is an ongoing cultural revival, and we welcome people to bring their influences here to grow the environment of Rojava.
